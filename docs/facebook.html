Facebook Dilemma

Why is the Facebook Dilemma a problem?

In the United States, Facebook's ability to manipulate news is hotly debated. According to James Jacoby, director of the latest Frontline documentary "The Facebook Dilemma," Facebook is almost the only source of news in certain parts of the world.

According to Jacoby, social media platforms like Facebook and Twitter were widely praised as "forces for good in the world" around 2011. In Egypt, Facebook was instrumental in rallying dissidents and organizing demonstrations, ultimately leading to President Hosni Mubarak's overthrow.

This is just one of the many examples of how sites like Facebook can influence and sway peoples opinions on certain topics.
